Banana Bread Muffins

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30–40 minutes
Servings: 12 muffins
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • ¼ cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, mix sugar, oil, and egg until light and creamy.
  3. Stir in mashed bananas and chopped walnuts.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt; mix until smooth.
  5. Spoon batter into muffin cups, filling each about 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 30–40 minutes, or until tops spring back when lightly pressed.
