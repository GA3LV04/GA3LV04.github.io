Gingerbread Cookies

Soft, spiced cookies perfect for any season.

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 24 cookies
Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and brown sugar together until smooth.
  3. Add molasses and egg and mix well.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, and spices.
  5. Gradually mix dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Roll dough out and cut into cookie shapes.
  7. Place cookies on a baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes until edges are firm.
  9. Let cookies cool before decorating.
