Gingerbread Cookies

Prep: 20 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Total: 2h 30m
Servings: 36

Ingredients

  • 3½ cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp cloves
  • ½ tsp salt

Directions

  1. Whisk flour, baking soda, and spices.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together.
  3. Add egg, molasses, and vanilla.
  4. Mix in flour mixture.
  5. Refrigerate dough 2 hours.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 8–10 minutes.
