Banana Bread

A delicious chocolate chip banana bread. Use ripe bananas for best result!

Ingredients

  • Banana Bread Ingredients
    • 3 ripe bananas
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 cup of melted butter
    • 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar
    • 1tsp baking powder
    • 1tsp baking soda
    • 1tsp cinammon
    • 1tsp of vanilla extract
    • 1 & 1/2 cup of flour
  • add chocolate chips to mix
  • preheat the oven to 335 degrees
  • bake for 50 minutes and check until its done!

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 355 degrees
  2. Mash three bananas in large bowl
  3. mix in 2 eggs
  4. add melted butter to bowl
  5. add 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar to bowl
  6. after mix in 1tsp of baking powder, baking soda, cinammon, and vanilla extract
  7. then add 1 & 1/2 cup of flour and mix and once you are done mixing add the chocolate chips. And now pour mixture into a non stick pan with parchment paper and throw into oven.
