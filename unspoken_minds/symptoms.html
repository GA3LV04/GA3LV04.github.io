Top 5 Symptoms

These are common signs of anxiety and depression that many young adults may experience.

1. Constant Worry or Overthinking

Anxiety can make thoughts feel difficult to control, especially about school, relationships, or the future.

2. Low Energy or Fatigue

Depression and anxiety can make everyday tasks feel exhausting, even after resting.

3. Trouble Sleeping

Some people may struggle to fall asleep, stay asleep, or may sleep too much and still feel tired.

4. Difficulty Concentrating

Mental health challenges can make schoolwork, reading, and daily responsibilities harder to manage.

5. Loss of Interest or Motivation

A person may stop enjoying hobbies, social events, or goals that used to matter to them.
