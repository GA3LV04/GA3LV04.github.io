Top 5 Symptoms

Anxiety and depression can affect people in different ways. These are five common symptoms that many young adults may experience.

1. Constant Worry or Overthinking

Many people with anxiety feel like their thoughts never slow down. They may constantly worry about school, relationships, the future, or things that have not even happened yet.

Learn More

2. Low Energy or Fatigue

Depression and anxiety can make everyday tasks feel exhausting. Even after resting, someone may still feel drained, unmotivated, or too tired to focus.

Learn More

3. Trouble Sleeping

Mental health struggles often affect sleep patterns. Some people may have insomnia, while others may sleep too much and still not feel rested.

Learn More

4. Difficulty Concentrating

Anxiety and depression can make it harder to focus in class, finish assignments, or stay organized. This can make school and daily responsibilities feel even more stressful.

Learn More

5. Loss of Motivation or Interest

A person may stop enjoying activities they used to like or feel disconnected from friends, hobbies, or school. This is a common sign of depression and emotional burnout.
