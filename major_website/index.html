Student Wellness

Why Mental Health Matters for Students

College can be one of the most challenging periods of your life. Between academic pressure, social dynamics, financial stress, and major life transitions, it's no wonder that anxiety and depression are increasingly common among young adults.

Understanding your mental health is not a sign of weakness—it's a sign of strength. By learning to recognize symptoms, develop coping strategies, and access support when needed, you're taking control of your wellbeing.

This resource is designed to help you navigate these challenges with evidence-based information, practical tools, and a reminder that you're not facing this alone.
